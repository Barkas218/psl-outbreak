There is a Tusavirus outbreak in Colombia. A national emergency has been declared by the government. The virus spreads quickly from person to person. Information on the evolution of the epidemic by department can be found on this website. The hypothesis that symptoms may be made worse by watching a music video or listening to a song is investigated. There are ongoing investigations to learn more.
